About the Game

Lust Legion is a hand-drawn Point & Click Visual Novel set in a vibrant university world.

Step into the shoes of a college student whose life is turned upside down by an experiment gone... interestingly wrong. While uncovering the truth behind your father's disappearance, you will navigate campus life, manage your finances, and build relationships with a cast of unique characters.

Your choices shape the story. How will you use your new power?
