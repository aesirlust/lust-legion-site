About Lust Legion

Lust Legion is an adult orientated high quality visual novel, currently in development. Set in a mysterious university, you play as a student navigating a world of secrets, power, and desire.

The game features over 20 characters to meet and interact with, unique locations, and a point & click inventory system. Uncover the truth about the Legion while managing your relationships and studies.

You can download the game for free, but by supporting on Patreon you gain exclusive rewards such as early access, walkthroughs, and high-resolution galleries.
