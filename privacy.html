Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

This website is static and hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers (names, emails, IP addresses, etc.). We do not have a database and we do not use tracking pixels or analytics software.

2. Local Storage & Cookies

We use a technology called Local Storage solely for the purpose of the "Age Gate" functionality. This saves a small piece of data on your device (`ageConfirmed = true`) to remember that you have verified your age, so you don't have to confirm it every time you visit. This data remains on your device and is never sent to us.

3. Third-Party Links

This site contains links to third-party platforms such as Patreon and Discord. Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those other sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

4. Contact

If you have questions, you can contact us via our official Patreon page.
